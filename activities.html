Activities to do nearby

Hiking

Lika offers numerous destinations and peaks for hiking. We suggest visiting nearby Mrsinj, Kremen, and Ozeblin. There are endless possibilities for hiking in Velebit or Gorski kotar nearby. You can see some possibilities on Croatian Mountaineering association page.

Cycling

There are scenic routes for experienced cyclists in Lika! Bikepackers are our usual guests and we love hearing experiences from the trip.

Plitvice lakes

Less than a 20-minute ride from Katarica house is the most famous National Park in Croatia, Plitvička jezera! There are many paths for walking, hiking, and enjoying the view of numerous lakes and waterfalls. It's a must-see when visiting Croatia! We suggest visiting their official website for more information and ticket reservations.

Cave exploring

There are many caves in the region. The closest is Baračeve caves and Cave park Grabovača.
You can find out more information on their official websites Baračeve caves and Cave park Grabovača.
